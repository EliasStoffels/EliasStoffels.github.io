About

Because of my interest in math and trying out new things I took the PPGA course at DAE where I made a game using PPGA (Plane Based Geometric Algebra) for movement instead of normal vector math.

All movement and rotation in this project is handled by mirroring the points over basis plains and their cross sections

For this project I used a PPGA Math library that handled basic PPGA types and operations
